Sam Rajib

Founder & CEO

With over 15 years of experience in the travel industry, Sam Rajib has transformed Trip Fly BD into one of Bangladesh's most trusted travel agencies. His vision for customer-centric service and attention to detail has earned the agency numerous accolades.
